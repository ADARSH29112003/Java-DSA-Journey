# Day 02 Interview Questions

## Q1

What is an operator?

Answer:

An operator is a symbol that performs an operation on values or variables.

---

## Q2

Difference between = and == ?

Answer:

= assigns a value.

== compares two values.

---

## Q3

Why does 5 / 2 print 2?

Answer:

Because both values are integers and Java performs integer division.

---

## Q4

What does % operator do?

Answer:

It returns the remainder after division.

Example:

17 % 5 = 2

---

## Q5

What do logical operators return?

Answer:

Boolean values.

true or false

---

## Q6

Difference between && and || ?

Answer:

&& requires all conditions to be true.

|| requires at least one condition to be true.
